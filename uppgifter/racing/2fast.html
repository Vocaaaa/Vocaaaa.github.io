Description

2Fast has firmly established its position on the automatic scooter market and is
now venturing out onto the market for geared mopeds. The 2Fast Cylinder Kit
100cc Derbi Euro 3 (D50B0) is a high-end aluminium cylinder with a very resistant
Nikasil coating accompanied by 2Fast's famous anodized blue cylinder head. It has
6 transfer ports and bridged exhaust port, Bore diameter is 52mm (Big Bore).

The cylinder has to be used with a crankshaft with 47mm stroke and 95mm
connecting rod; this will add up to a powerful displacement of 100cc. The cylinder
provides the option to mount different exhaust types, with 2-hole or 3-hole flanges.

  • aluminium cylinder, 52mm bore
  • 1-ring piston, 14mm pin
  • 47mm stroke, 95mm connecting rod
  • crankcase milling is necessary
  • not street-legal
