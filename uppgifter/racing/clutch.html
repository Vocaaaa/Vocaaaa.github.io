Description

Clutch Discs 6 discs Stage6 R/T Derbi, 6 disc set from Stage6 R/T for 50cc mopeds
(with gearbox) with Derbi engine.

Stage6 R/T is the high-end tuning department of Stage6, and accordingly this 6-
disc clutch was developed and built for powerful high-end racing engines with 25
HP and more. What you usually have with very powerful engines is that the original
clutch is prone to slip, the engine's output is not effectively transmitted to the
wheel, and this means you will have to upgrade the clutch.

The clutch consists of 6 friction discs with a special organic aramid-reinforced
lining, 5 plain discs and 5 clutch springs. The high-quality components guarantee a
very long service life and durability and a significant reduction of clutch slip even
with very powerful engines.

Tip: Before mounting, soak the discs in gear oil overnight.

  • for Derbi Euro 2 / Euro 3 / Euro 4 engines
  • kickstarter can be retained
  • high-end racing clutch
