Description

MVT Internal Rotor Ignition "Digital Direct" with lighting coil Minarelli AM6, high-end
racing ignition with its easily recognizable orange stator and black rotor.

The MVT Digital Direct ignitions feature variable ignition timing controlled by a CDI
unit (included), meaning that ignition advance is rev-dependent in order to max out performance.
The HT coil provides a discharge current of up to 42,000 V at high rpm. In addition, the Digital Direct
features a 12 V supply for lighting or the electric water pump which is not common for most ignitions.

Note: you really have to make sure what kind of vehicle you have (year, model) before ordering this ignition as it comes in several versions.
This one is compatible with Minarelli engines without wire harness (and without battery). For geared mopeds
with Minarelli AM6 engine EXCEPT MBK / Yamaha after 2003.

You cannot use an electric choke with this ignition!
