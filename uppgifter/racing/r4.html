Description

Yasuni Exhaust R4 black Minarelli AM6, result of Mopojonnet's successful
collaboration with Spanish manufacturer Yasuni. Yasuni got together with
Mopojonnet's racing department "MXS Racing" to develop this incredibly powerful
exhaust system.

Specifically developed to go with 84cc - 94cc "Hyper Racing" cylinders, it is very
versatile and can be combined with most large displacement engines. Given its
rather large size, we recommend combining it with a larger carburetor (at least
28mm).

Yasuni Exhaust R4 with black aluminium silencer Derbi / Gilera => reserved for the
race track, NOT street-legal!
